🎉 Welcome to IBS4 Post-Internship Hub!

First of all, big congratulations on starting your IBS4 journey and securing you internship — exciting times ahead! 🙌

This website is here to help guide you through everything you need to prepare and submit for your internship documentation, all in one place.

You’ll need to have four key documents ready by the start of your 5th semester:

  • Internship Certificate
  • Internship Presentation
  • Internship Report
  • Internship Paper
You can find detailed explanations and tips on the Documents page.

This portal is designed to make the process clearer and smoother for you — so you can focus on making the most of your internship and gaining valuable real-world experience

Wishing you a great internship and a smooth journey ahead. You’ve got this! 💪
